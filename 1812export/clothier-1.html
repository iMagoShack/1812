As the Creative Director and President of 1812 Society, Patrick wears many hats.  His primary focuses are on company growth strategies and servicing his long list of clients, as well as day to day operations and creative projects. He leverages years of experience in custom clothing and working with his highly affluent clients to push 1812 ahead one custom suit at a time. He lives in downtown Columbus, OH, but frequently travels to most major cities for private appointments with business owners, celebrity stylists, and professional athletes. Patrick’s strengths are total closet solutions and evening wear, especially weddings and coordinating the clothing for large events.
