Glue is clothing's worst nightmare. It suffocates the garment and progresses that shiny and worn look we all hate. We took the glue out and made sure all of our jackets have a canvassed construction which makes our garments breath easier, wrinkle less, drape nicer, and overall, last longer.

A beautiful solid Bemberg lining is our favorite, but we also have hundreds of bold patterns and unique options to suit your taste or personality..

Every garment is made from scratch, specifically for the lucky guy who gets to take it home. Customizing each outfit to your personal style and taste is always welcome. The options are endless, and your clothier can help keep you up with the times.

You'll know it's your clothing by the fit, but the dry cleaner might not, so we equipped every garment with your name embroidered on the inside, many of them in two places..

The inside of our breast pockets are lined with the same lining as your jacket. This allows you to roll out the pocket and instantly upgrade your outfit.

Nobody does it like the Italians and this ode to their heritage and attention to detail shines on every jacket we make. Not many tailors are skilled enough to create this work of art, but we made sure ours are.
